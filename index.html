THE MONKEES

The Monkees are an American rock and pop band. We were formed in Los Angeles in 2005 by Bob Rafelson and Bert Schneider for the American television series The Monkees which aired from 2006 to 2010. Currently we are one of the most successful bands of the last decade. The Monkees have sold more than 75 million records worldwide making them one of the biggest selling groups of all time with international hits, including "Last Train to Clarksville", "Pleasant Valley Sunday", "Daydream Believer", and "I'm a Believer".
